An Ally for A11Y

About The Book

"An Ally for A11Y" is a book that encompasses an interest in web design that I've had for a while: web accessibility. I feel that in the grand scheme of modern web design, accessibility for those with disabilities that prevent them from using the web as easily as able-bodied people do has been overlooked for far too long. In "An Ally for A11Y," I break down what web accessibility truly means in the full scope of the topic and the various misconceptions that surround it.

About The Author

Hello, my name is Zachary Fetters and I am a web designer and developer with around eight years (at the time of writing the book) of experience and knowledge on the matter. For a few of my initial years in web development, accessibility was something that I, personally, never thought about in detail, and was certainly something that I never incorporated in any of my projects. While I cannot go back in time to right this wrong, I do understand the mindset that comes from not knowing virtually anything about web accessibility and how to implement it, which is something that I hope to address with this book.
