About Me

My name is Zachary Fetters and I am a freelance web and graphic designer. I've been designing since 2011 and I have started freelancing in September of 2020 in the hopes to build upon my client repertoire and gain professional experience in designing. My design focus is built upon strong, flashy colors and minimal presentation that isn't afraid to play with palettes and shapes.

My Projects

Fora

Fora is an open-source, free/libre web software for communities. Fora aims to fuse the community features of Reddit, as well as the organization of the common forum to provide the best experience for communities of any size and purpose. Fora's purpose is to remove the toxic, unconducive features of many social media sites to prioritize and promote dialog and communication between community member.
